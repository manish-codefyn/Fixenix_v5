Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of mobile repairs do you offer?
    We offer repairs for screen issues, battery replacement, charging port problems, and more.
  • How long does a mobile repair take?
    Most repairs are completed within 1-2 hours.
  • Do I need to make an appointment?
    Appointments are recommended but not required. Walk-ins are welcome.

  • What desktop issues can you fix?
    We handle hardware malfunctions, software issues, and network problems.
  • Is data recovery available?
    Yes, we offer data recovery services for damaged or corrupted hard drives.

  • Do you repair both Windows and Mac laptops?
    Yes, we repair both Windows and Mac laptops.
  • What should I do before bringing my laptop in for repair?
    Please back up your data and remove any sensitive information.

  • Can you repair any brand of CCTV camera?
    Yes, we can repair most major CCTV camera brands.
  • What should I do if my CCTV system is not recording?
    Check the connections and power supply. If the issue persists, bring it in for a diagnostic.

  • What other electronics do you repair?
    We repair a variety of electronics including tablets, gaming consoles, and more.
  • How can I get a quote for a repair?
    Contact us through our website or visit us in person for a detailed quote.
